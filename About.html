About Us

Location: Athletic Center

Date: Every other Friday

Time: 6:30pm - 8:30pm

Email: Weight.lifting@gmail.com

The Weightlifting Club is a community dedicated to helping students build strength, confidence, and camaraderie through the discipline of weightlifting. Open to all skill levels, we provide structured training programs, expert guidance, and a supportive environment where members can achieve their fitness goals. Whether you're new to lifting or an experienced athlete, our club is here to empower you to grow both physically and mentally while fostering lasting friendships. Join us to lift, learn, and excel together!
